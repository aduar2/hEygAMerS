1. Cranium

This is a game I play with my family pretty frequently. It'a combo of a few other party games, which makes it super fun. There are 4 decks of cards, each one a different yet similar game. The Creative Cat cards give you a prompt to draw, and you play through much like Pictionary. that you have to draw and make people guess like Pictionary. Players roll the dice and move their customizable character across the board. First one to the brain at the center wins It's for 4-16 people. Unfortunately, this game is quite pricy. With a suggested retail price of 49.99, its probably only worth it if you know you're going to play it. It's quite worth it if you have a lot of friends or family
